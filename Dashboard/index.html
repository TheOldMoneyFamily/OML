About AI Academy

Our Mission & Vision

AI Academy is dedicated to empowering individuals and organizations with the knowledge and tools to thrive in the AI-powered world. We believe in democratizing AI education and making it accessible to everyone. Our vision is to foster a future where AI is used for good, driving innovation and progress across all sectors.

Meet Our Founder & CEO

Arifur Rahman Chowdhury is a highly skilled and experienced professional with a proven track record in web development, accounting software, YouTube content creation, and consulting. His passion for technology and numbers drives him to create efficient, user-friendly solutions that meet and exceed client expectations. He brings a deep understanding of both the technical and business aspects of AI, making him uniquely positioned to lead AI Academy in its mission to empower the world with AI knowledge.

Our Team & Expertise

Beyond our founder, AI Academy is comprised of a dedicated team of AI instructors, developers, and specialists who are passionate about sharing their knowledge and fostering a community of AI learners. We believe that AI should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background or experience.

Partnerships & Collaborations

AI Academy is committed to collaboration. We work closely with leading AI institutions, research centers, and technology companies to ensure our curriculum and resources remain at the forefront of the field.
